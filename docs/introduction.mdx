# Orbit: Your AI Company's Operating Layer

> One place where your AI agents share memory, file their work, and operate from a single source of truth — so you can run a real company with AI, not just a collection of disconnected tools.

---

## What is Orbit?

If you're running more than one AI agent — whether that's Claude handling your research, Codex writing your code, or an n8n workflow managing your outreach — you've probably hit the same wall:

**Each agent starts every session from zero. They don't know what the others figured out. They don't remember what you decided last week. And you have no idea what any of them are doing right now.**

Orbit fixes this. It's the shared layer your agents read from and write to — so your entire AI operation compounds over time instead of starting fresh every run.

---

## The three things Orbit gives you

### 1. Shared Memory (The Vault)
Every output your agents produce gets stored in the Orbit Vault — reports, decisions, research, notes. Every agent can search this vault before acting. So when your sales agent runs today, it already knows what your research agent found yesterday. Nothing gets lost between tools.

### 2. A Live Dashboard
Open Orbit and see exactly what every agent is doing, what they've produced, and what needs your attention. Your Briefing page gives you a CEO-style morning brief every day. Your Inbox shows what's new and needs review. Your org chart shows your whole AI workforce at a glance.

### 3. Decisions That Stick
File a decision once — "always use this pricing," "don't contact this lead," "the auth flow works like this" — and every agent reads it before every run. Change the rule once; every future run picks it up automatically. No more re-explaining the same thing to every new chat.

---

## Who is Orbit for?

**Solo founders building with AI** — You're running multiple Claude agents, maybe Cursor for code, maybe n8n for automation. You need them to work as a team, not as isolated tools. Orbit is your company's operating layer.

**AI power users** — You live in Claude, ChatGPT, and other AI tools daily. You're tired of every new chat forgetting everything the last one figured out. Orbit gives all your tools a shared memory.

**Lean teams** — Two to five people, all using AI. No shared context, no visibility into what agents decided. Orbit gives your team one place where everything lands.

---

## What Orbit is not

Orbit is not another AI chatbot. It's not a prompt tool. It's not a note-taking app.

It's the coordination layer that sits underneath your entire AI operation — reading, writing, governing, and surfacing everything your agents do, so you can run your business instead of managing your tools.

---

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Orbit workspace">
    Sign up at [app.orbitagents.xyz](https://app.orbitagents.xyz). Your workspace is where your agents, vault, and dashboard live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your first agent">
    Add your Orbit MCP endpoint to your AI tool of choice. Takes about 2 minutes. See the [Quickstart](/docs/quickstart) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch your agents check in">
    Once connected, your agent will register with Orbit at the start of every run — appearing on your dashboard, reading active decisions, and filing its outputs to your vault automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your AI company">
    Add more agents, assign them to departments, give them roles. Your org chart builds itself as agents connect. Your vault grows smarter every day.
  </Step>
</Steps>
