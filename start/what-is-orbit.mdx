Orbit gives every AI tool and agent a shared brain: one memory to read from, one place to file work, and one live dashboard to manage it all. It replaces the friction of copy-pasting context manually, shuffling between Claude, Codex, Cursor, and removes you and your computer from being the context layer. You and your whole AI workforce connect to the same Orbit Cloud, see, manage, and write before any action so your AI life stays synced in real-time. 

## The problem Orbit solves

Right now, every AI tool you use starts from zero. Every scheduled Claude agent starts from zero and doesn't know what it did last run. Claude Cowork doesn't know what Claude Code did or what Codex did. You repeat yourself with no visibility and coordination; hence, you become busy managing all the context... 

Orbit ends that. Every session you work in or your scheduled agent, whether it's on Claude, Codex, Cursor (we're adding new ones consistently), checks into the same workspace before it acts. It reads what you've decided, sees what you and your other agents have already done, and files what it produces back to one shared vault. You stop being the messenger between your tools, and your AI work finally starts compounding instead of resetting. One place to see everything, remember what matters, and stay in control.

## How Orbit works

Orbit sits in the middle as a shared layer every agent connects to:

1. **Connect every AI tool you use**\
   Claude, Cursor, Codex, ChatGPT, Gemini, n8n — anything that speaks MCP plugs into Orbit in minutes. Orbit sits on top of the execution while it takes place where it already is. One workspace, every tool you already work in connected.
2. **Everything you and your agents do lands in one place**\
   Every conversation you have. Every output your agents produce. Every decision you make. All of it is filed automatically to your Vault — searchable, timestamped, organized by project, attributed to whoever produced it.
3. **Your tools start every session already knowing what matters**\
   Before any new chat, new agent run, or new session begins, Orbit hands it the context that matters (authoritative knowledge \+ relevant search) — your active decisions, your prior conclusions, what other agents have already figured out. No more starting from zero. No more re-explaining yourself. Have a new updated positioning? Hand it off to your agent.
4. **Your Inbox updated in real-time**\
   Open Orbit and see what happened across all your AI work overnight — your scheduled agents, your background workflows, your prior sessions. One card per agent or session. Read it with your coffee. Done in 4 minutes.
5. **Decisions stick across every tool, every session**\
   File a decision once — "use the pricing document," "don't touch the module v2," "the brand voice sounds like this" — and every connected tool reads it before every run. Forever. The work compounds instead of resetting.
6. **You stay in control**\
   Knowledge conflicts get flagged before they become problems. As work compounds over time, trust levels keep speculation separate from ground truth. You approve what becomes canonical. The tools do the work. You run the show.

## Who it's for

Solopreneurs running five AI tools at once. Founders building an AI-native team. Operators who live inside Claude all day. If your work is spread across multiple agents and you're tired of being the memory between them, Orbit is built for you.
