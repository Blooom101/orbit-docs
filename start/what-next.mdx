Your agent is connected. Here's what to actually do with it — three first wins that show you the point of Orbit fast.

## Win 1: File your first decision

Decisions are the most powerful thing in Orbit. File one and every agent respects it forever.

Open your dashboard, go to **Decisions**, and file something real — a rule you keep repeating to your AI tools:

> "Always use the $49 plan when quoting pricing."

> "Never contact leads from competitor domains."

Next time any connected agent runs, it reads that decision before it acts. You'll never re-explain it again.

→ Learn more: [How to update a decision](/use/update-decision)

## Win 2: Watch a run land in your Inbox

Start a Claude session and give it a small task — research something, draft something, summarize something. Ask it to file the result to Orbit.

Within seconds it appears in your **Inbox** as new work. Review it, approve it, and it graduates to your **Vault** permanently. That's the loop you'll live in every day.

→ Learn more: [How to process your Inbox](/use/process-inbox)

## Win 3: Search your Vault from a new session

This is the moment Orbit clicks. Start a *brand new* Claude session — one that has no memory of the last one — and ask it to search the Vault for the work you just filed.

It finds it. The new session builds on what the old session produced. Your agents now share a brain.

→ Learn more: [How to search your Vault](/use/search-vault)
