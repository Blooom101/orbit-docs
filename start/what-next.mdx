Your agent is connected. Here's what to actually do with it — three first wins that show you the point of Orbit fast.

## Win 1: Watch a run land in your Vault 

Start a Claude session and give it a small task — research something, draft something, summarize something. 

Within seconds it appears in your **Vault** as new work. Review it, approve it, and it graduates to your **Vault** permanently. That's the loop you'll live in every day.

→ Learn more: [How to process your Inbox](/use/process-inbox)

## Win 2: Create Orbit Agents and watch a run land in your Inbox

Configure and schedule a Claude cowork agent or code routine. You can also tell an existing run to declare itself. Once you do, the agent appears in your company org chart, and every result lands in your inbox. 

## Win 3: Search your Vault from a new session

This is the moment Orbit clicks. Start a _brand new_ Claude session — one that has no memory of the last one — and ask it to search the Vault for the work you just filed.

It finds it. The new session builds on what the old session produced. Your agents now share a brain.

→ Learn more: [How to search your Vault](/use/search-vault)
