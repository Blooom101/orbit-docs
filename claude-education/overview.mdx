You don't need to be technical to get a lot out of Claude. This short track walks you through using it like a pro — in plain language, one idea at a time — with Orbit quietly handling the parts that used to be hard.

## Who this is for

You're running a business mostly on your own. You use AI to move faster, but you're not a developer and you don't want to be. You want Claude to feel less like a clever chatbot and more like a small team that remembers what you told it.

That's exactly what this track sets you up for.

## The one idea behind everything

Claude on its own is brilliant but forgetful. Every new chat starts from a blank page — it doesn't remember yesterday, and your different AI tools don't talk to each other.

**Orbit is the memory.** Once it's connected, Claude reads what you've already decided before it does anything, and saves what it produces so the next time picks up where you left off. You stop being the sticky-note between your tools.

Keep that picture in mind and the rest is easy.
