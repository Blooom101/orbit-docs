## How pricing is structured

Orbit is priced around how much of an AI workforce you're running — roughly, the number of connected agents and the volume of work they file. The more your operation scales, the higher the tier.

- **Solo** — for an individual running a handful of agents across their own tools.
- **Team** — for founders building an AI-native team with multiple departments and projects.
- **Enterprise** — for larger operations, with options like [self-hosting](/reference/self-hosted), advanced security, and dedicated support.
