---
title: "How to read your morning Briefing"
description: "Catch up on everything your AI workforce did overnight."
---

Your Briefing is the first thing to open each morning. It shows one card per agent summarizing what each one did while you were away.

## Steps

1. Open your Orbit dashboard. The **Briefing** is your default landing view.
2. Scan the agent cards top to bottom. Each shows the agent's name, a one-line summary of its last run, and when it ran.
3. Click any card to expand the full list of what that agent filed — reports, notes, decisions.
4. Click through to any individual item to read it in full.

## What to look for

- **Agents that didn't run** when you expected them to — a sign something's blocked.
- **Unusually short summaries** — the agent may have hit an error or had nothing to do.
- **Anything that needs your input** — those items also appear in your [Inbox](/use/process-inbox), which is where you'll act on them.

## Briefing vs. Inbox

The Briefing is read-only — it's for *visibility*. When you actually need to review, approve, or decide something, switch to the [Inbox](/use/process-inbox). Think of the Briefing as the headlines and the Inbox as your action queue.
