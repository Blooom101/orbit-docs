---
title: "How to organize your org chart"
description: "Structure your AI workforce visually."
---

The org chart assembles itself as agents connect, but you shape it by assigning roles, departments, and leads.

## Steps

1. Open the **Org Chart** from your dashboard.
2. Click the agent and select department, set each agent's department from its detail panel.
3. Designate a **department lead** where it helps — useful when one agent coordinates others.
4. Review the layout: every department, every agent, every shift status in one view.

## What you can see at a glance

- Which departments exist and how many agents are in each
- The lead of each department
- Which agents are on shift vs. idle
- The relationships between agents

## Keep it current

As you add agents, assign them to departments right away so the chart stays an accurate map of your operation. An out-of-date org chart is worse than none — it tells you the wrong thing at a glance.
