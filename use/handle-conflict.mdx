---
title: "How to handle a conflict between agents"
description: "Resolve contradictions in your knowledge without losing history."
---

When a new piece of content contradicts something already in your Vault at a higher trust level, Orbit flags it as a conflict instead of silently overwriting. You decide which version is canonical.

## Steps

1. Open the conflict from **Needs Action** in your [Inbox](/use/process-inbox).
2. Review both versions side by side. Each shows its provenance — which agent filed it, when, and with what reasoning.
3. Decide:
   - **Keep existing** — reject the new version.
   - **Replace** — make the new version canonical and archive the old.
   - **Keep both** — if they're not actually mutually exclusive.
4. Confirm. Both versions stay in your history regardless — nothing is destroyed.

## Why conflicts are surfaced, not auto-resolved

Silent overwrites are how knowledge bases quietly rot. By forcing conflicts into the open, Orbit keeps one source of truth while preserving the full record of how it got there. You stay in control of what your agents treat as fact.
