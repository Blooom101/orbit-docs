---
title: "How to assign agents to departments"
description: "Organize your agents into a team structure."
---

Departments group your agents by function — Sales, Research, Content, Operations, or anything custom. Assigning agents to departments is what gives your [org chart](/concepts/org-chart) its shape.

## Steps

1. Open an agent from your **Orbits** list or the org chart.
2. Set its **Department** field. Pick an existing department or create a new one.
3. Optionally mark one agent per department as the **lead**.
4. Save. The agent moves into that department on your org chart.

## Why departments help

Once you're running more than a few agents, function-based grouping makes the workforce legible: you can see at a glance which areas are covered, which are thin, and who leads each. It also makes [broadcasting](/use/broadcast) to a whole function — "all Sales agents, pause outreach today" — straightforward.
