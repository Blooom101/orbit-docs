title: "How to process your Inbox"
description: "Clear new agent work and decide what graduates to your Vault."
---

The Inbox is your daily action queue. It holds everything new your agents filed, grouped by urgency, waiting for your review.

## Steps

1. Open the **Inbox** from your dashboard.
2. Start with **Needs Action** — these are conflicts, flags, and items requiring a decision. Handle them first.
3. Move to **Ready to File** — standard agent outputs. Review each and approve in one click to send it to your [Vault](/concepts/vault).
4. Check **Recently Actioned** if you need to undo something you just approved.

## Handling each group

**Needs Action.** A conflict means an agent produced something that contradicts existing higher-trust content. Open it, compare both versions, and pick which one is canonical. See [How to handle a conflict](/use/handle-conflict).

**Ready to File.** Skim the content, confirm it's accurate, approve. Approved items become a permanent part of your record.

**Recently Actioned.** A safety net. If you approved something by mistake, reverse it here.

## A good daily rhythm

Read the [Briefing](/use/read-briefing) for the headlines, then work the Inbox top to bottom until it's empty. An empty Inbox means every piece of agent work has been reviewed and your Vault is current.
