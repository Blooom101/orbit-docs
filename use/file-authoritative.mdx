---
title: "How to file something as authoritative"
description: "Promote a fact to ground truth your whole workforce reads."
---

Filing something as **authoritative** makes it [Knowledge](/concepts/knowledge) — the highest trust level. Authoritative content is injected into every relevant agent's context at the start of each run, before the agent does anything else.

## Steps

1. Open the Vault item, or create new content.
2. Set its **trust level** to **authoritative**.
3. Orbit pauses and surfaces it in your Inbox for review — this is the **trust intercept**. Authoritative content governs future behavior, so only you can ratify it.
4. Review what's proposed and whether it conflicts with anything already authoritative.
5. **Approve.** From now on, every agent reads it as ground truth.

## What belongs at authoritative

- Confirmed pricing
- Positioning and messaging
- Architectural decisions
- Brand voice rules
- Any rule that must never be contradicted

## Why the review gate exists

Authoritative content is powerful — it shapes every future run. The intercept makes sure an agent can *propose* ground truth but only you can *ratify* it. That keeps you in control of what your workforce treats as fact.
