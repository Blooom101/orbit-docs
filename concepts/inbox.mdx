The Inbox is your daily ops surface. It shows every output and report from your agents that's new and hasn't been reviewed yet.

## Inbox and Vault are not completely the same

The Inbox is just a filtered view of the Vaults agent outputs. When something arrives from an agent, it shows up in your Inbox as new. Once you've reviewed or approved it, it's marked as seen and stays in the Vault permanently. Vault, on the other hand, also shows all the outputs from your sessions. The work you did with AI. 

## Grouped by urgency

- **Needs Action** — conflicts detected, flags raised, items needing your decision
- **Ready to File** — standard agent outputs you can approve in one click
- **Recently Actioned** — in case you want to undo something

## Why it works this way

Agents produce a lot. If everything were dumped straight into permanent memory with no review, your Vault would fill with unverified output.
